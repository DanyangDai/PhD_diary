Updated Pre-Omicron cohort to 6 months

Both Omicron and Pre-cohort table 1s are ready to go. See dropboxs and UQ_ISARIC_Omicron.

Looked up paper: An international observational study to assess the impact of the Omicron variant emergence on the clinical epidemiology of COVID-19 in hospitalised patients which uses the same dataset. They defined the Pre-Omicron period as two-month prior 10% of Omicron. Omicron period as two-month post 90% of Omicron for each country. That means each country has a different Omicron and Pre-Omicron period. I pulled the data from Github repo: globaldothealth /covid19-variants-summary where they have the start date on when there are more than 90% of Omicron variant for each country.
